This page is for everyone who are interested in knowing what the School of Information Technology and Engineering (SITE) of KBTU is.

There is our Mission: School of Information Technology and Engineering is, first of all, a family of like-minded people, whose goal is to bring even more happiness and pleasure to society through education: so that every year there will be several hundred more young people in the intellectual layer of society who can make responsible decisions for the well-being of their families, for companies in which they work and for society as a whole. Today SITE is the largest school of KBTU, where more than two thousand students of bachelors, masters and doctoral students receive educational services.

There is a short overview for 5 programs of the SITE.

Code of the Program Program Name School subjects to pass on the exam Description
6B07103 Automation and Control Math+Physics Prepares specialists in the design and construction of automation devices and computer systems.
6B06101 Information System Math+Informatics The aim of the program is to train qualified specialists in the development and maintenance of information systems, able to analyze and identify business opportunities.
6B06102 Computer System and Software (CSS) Math+Informatics This specialty covers a wide range, from theoretical and algorithmic foundations to advanced developments in both computing and software.
6B06104 Management in IT Math+Informatics The purpose of the Program is to train qualified specialists in the development and maintenance of information systems in the field of IT management, management of the development of an organization by IT processes.
6B07114 Robotics and mechatronics Math+Physics The purpose of the Program is to train qualified specialists in the development and maintenance of information systems in the field of IT management, management of the development of an organization by IT processes.
